About me

My name is Peni Mitchell Shute. I am 30 years of age, and I am originally from Savusavu in Vanua Levu. I have maternal links to the Chiefly Village of Tubou, Lakeba in Lau.

I am an experienced Corporate Communications Specialist with a demonstrated history of working in the water utility industry. I have skill sets in Communications Planning, Crisis Communications, Communication, Media Relations, and Strategic Communications. Strong media and communication professional with a Certificate IV in Media & Journalism and Public Relations, which is focused on Communication and Media Studies from Fiji National University.

I am currently the Acting Manager Communications & Stakeholder Engagement at the Water Authority of Fiji (WAF). I have been employed by WAF for the past nine (9) years.
